About

E-Summit 2027 is the flagship entrepreneurship event of MANIT Bhopal, bringing together innovators, startups, and industry leaders under one platform.

The summit aims to foster a culture of innovation and problem-solving by providing students with opportunities to learn, build, and connect. Through competitions, workshops, and networking sessions, participants gain real-world exposure to the startup ecosystem.
